Working with Lan-bridge

Lan-bridge is always on the look out for experienced,talented bilingual sales staff, translators,interpreters and trainers who can contribute to our success,in London or our Chinese offices.When we are hiring for an in-house role details will available on this website but we do also welcome speculative applications.We are a growing company and there are plenty of opportunities for career development and training.

Potential translators and interpreters

If you are interested in providing translation,proofreading or other services, please send us

  • A detailed CV including your current location
  • Proof of professional qualifications and accreditaions to uk@lan-bridge.com and we will get back to you.

Partnerships

Lan-bridge also welcomes partnerships with language services, localization and consulting companies working in China and internationally. Do get in touch if you are interested in working with Lan-bridge as a partner. Again, please contact uk@lan-bridge.com or use any of our other contact methods to get in touch.

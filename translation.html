Mandarin Chinese Translations

We know you want to make an impact in China, but the Chinese market can be intimidating for newcomers and old-hands alike. Because of our Chinese expertise, we’re able to give a better service than generalist translation agencies. We can check your content and advise on how to approach your translation, pointing out sections and ideas that might not resonate with the Chinese reader and helping you change them, so you know you’ll get a translation that truly creates value and ROI, rather than just costing money.

We have over 15 years' experience translating Mandarin Chinese into and out of all major world languages.Quality and accuracy has always been our top priority.

We employ over 100 in-house Chinese translators who have all been through our rigorous testing and training process , as well as talented proofreaders, designers, and quality assurance managers. Based in China, they're up to date with the latest lingo and trends.

Our experienced project managers make sure your translation gets the attention it deserves. From pre-processing, to translation, proofreading and typesetting, they make the whole process run like a well oiled machine.

We don't just understand China, we understand the rest of the world too. Our international cultural expertise means that nothing gets lost in translation, and your core messages get across intact.

Our IT team provides us with a secure network (we're ISO27001 accredited) as well as project management, CAT and terminology tools, and website build, meaning we're as efficient as possible, lowering costs and providing you with a quicker turnaround.

We know you've heard it before, but we really do love to go the extra mile for our clients. And with our UK office and staff, we're able to communnicate with you fast, clearly and easily.

Adapting your content for China

We will look at your project and identify areas that need adapting to meet the expectations and needs of Chinese users.Will the benefits of your project or service resonate with Chinese?Is there any background konwledge that we need to explain?Do we need to localise any multimedia, formats or code?

We adapt the translation so that it makes an impact with Chinese audiences by getting creative with certain words/sections or documents. For example, if you had a health care monitoring product aimed at middle class,middle-aged consumers which suggests gardening as a gentle and accesible way to stay healthy and active,we would know that the target consumers in China on the whole do not have gardens and that the suggestion would fail flat.Depending on the exact type of consumer, we might change it to an activity that reonsates with family-oriented Chinese consumers -perhaps playing with grandchildren-or a traditional Chinese exercise like tai chi.

We offer a range of services to make sure that your content will work best in the Chinese market.Depending on your needs,we can research keywords and carry our on-page search engine optimisation.We can also provide copywriting for when you need original content that taps in the Chinese zeitgeist,for example,for blogs,social media or advertising.We can even help you find appropriate names for your brand services or products.
